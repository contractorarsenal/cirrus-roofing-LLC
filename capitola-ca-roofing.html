Roofing Contractor in
Capitola, CA.

Roof repair, replacement and leak repair for Capitola's dense, close-to-the-coast residential neighborhoods.

Capitola is one of the more densely built coastal communities in the county, with homes often close together and many built decades ago, closer to the water than most of the surrounding area. That combination — older roofing stock and direct coastal exposure — means Capitola properties tend to face wear patterns that show up earlier than they would further inland.

Cirrus Roofing works on composition, tile and flat roofing throughout Capitola, from the Village area to the residential streets above it. Flashing condition and drainage tend to be the first things worth checking on older Capitola roofs, given how much salt-laden air and moisture these properties are exposed to.

Local Conditions

Coastal exposure on older roofs.

Capitola's proximity to the water means salt-laden air is a constant presence, which accelerates corrosion on standard metal flashing and fasteners faster than it would on a property even a few miles inland. On older roofs, that often shows up first as rust streaking or a failed flashing seal around chimneys and vents.

Because many homes here were built some time ago, roofing material that's simply aging combined with coastal moisture is a common combination we see. Drainage matters too — closely spaced homes and smaller lots mean water management around gutters and downspouts deserves real attention, not an afterthought.

Common Questions

Capitola roofing FAQs.

Do you work in the Capitola Village area, or just the residential streets above it?+
Both. We work throughout Capitola, from properties near the Village down to the residential neighborhoods further up the hill.
Why does salt air matter so much for Capitola roofs specifically?+
Capitola sits close enough to the water that airborne salt accelerates corrosion on standard metal flashing and fasteners faster than it would a few miles inland — it's worth factoring into material and hardware choices.
My roof is original to the house and the house is decades old — is it worth repairing or replacing?+
It depends on the extent of wear. We'll inspect and give you a straight comparison of repair versus replacement cost rather than assuming one answer for every older Capitola roof.
