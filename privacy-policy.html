Privacy
Policy.

How Cirrus Roofing collects, uses and protects information submitted through this website.

Contact and estimate form data. When you submit an estimate request or contact form on this site, the information you provide (name, phone number, email if given, property address or city, and project details) is sent to Cirrus Roofing so we can follow up about your request. We do not sell this information.

Third-party form processing. Estimate and contact forms on this site are processed through Web3Forms, a third-party form-delivery service that forwards your submission to our inbox. Web3Forms may process submission metadata as part of delivering the message. We do not control Web3Forms' own data practices beyond this delivery function.

Phone and email contact. If you call or email us directly using the contact information on this site, that communication is handled the same way any direct phone call or email would be — we use it to respond to you and do not share it with unrelated third parties.

Email inquiries. If you contact us by email, we use the email address and message content only to respond to your inquiry.

Analytics. If and when analytics tracking (such as Google Analytics) is enabled on this site, it may collect standard usage data such as pages visited, approximate location, device type and referring site. As of this policy's publication, analytics tracking has not yet been enabled; this section will be updated if that changes.

Advertising tracking. If advertising conversion tracking (such as Google Ads) is enabled in the future, it may use cookies or similar identifiers to measure ad performance. No advertising tracking is currently active on this site.

Cookies. This site may use minimal browser storage (such as session storage) to remember where a visitor came from (for example, which ad or link led to the site) so that information can be included with an estimate request. This data stays in your browser and is only sent to us if you submit a form.

Data retention. Submitted form data is retained only as long as needed to respond to your request and keep records of past customer communications.

Questions about this policy. If you have questions about how your information is handled, please call us at (408) 670-3199.
