Roof repair, replacement and leak repair for Monterey homes, where coastal fog and salt exposure shape material choice and maintenance.

Monterey sits directly on the coast, and its roofing stock reflects that — tile, metal and composition systems on properties that range from modest homes to larger, architecturally distinct houses across the Peninsula. Persistent coastal fog and marine layer are a near-daily presence here, more consistent than in some of the inland parts of the service area.

Cirrus Roofing works on tile, metal, composition and flat roofing across Monterey, with flashing and drainage detail getting particular attention given how much moisture and salt exposure these roofs deal with over time.

Local Conditions

Fog, salt air and architectural detail.

Near-daily fog keeps Monterey roofs damp for a large part of the year, which is a major factor in moss and algae growth on shaded or north-facing roof sections. It's also a contributor to slower drying after rain, which matters for flat and low-slope sections in particular.

Salt-air corrosion is a real consideration for metal flashing and fasteners on Monterey properties, especially those closer to the water. Standard hardware corrodes faster here than a few miles inland, which is worth factoring into repair and replacement material choices rather than defaulting to whatever's cheapest.
