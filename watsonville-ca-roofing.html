Local Conditions

What Watsonville roofs deal with.

Watsonville sits in an area with real seismic activity and wind loads that can reach the 90 mph range during winter storms, so roof structure and fastening matter as much as the surface material. Expansive soils in parts of the Pajaro Valley can also affect structural movement over time, which shows up as stress cracking on rigid roofing materials like tile.

Coastal fog and morning marine layer create ongoing moisture exposure, which encourages moss and algae growth on shaded roof sections — a problem that's increasingly flagged by homeowners insurance carriers during policy renewal inspections.
