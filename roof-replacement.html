Roof Replacement

Roof Replacement in
Watsonville, CA.

When repairs no longer make sense, a full roof replacement protects your home for decades. We install composition, metal, tile and flat roofing systems across Santa Cruz County to Monterey County.

Many homes across Watsonville, Santa Cruz and the surrounding coastal communities were built or last re-roofed decades ago, and a lot of that roofing material is now at or past its expected lifespan. Once a roof reaches that point, repeated small repairs stop being the economical choice — a full replacement becomes the better long-term investment.

Cirrus Roofing handles complete roof replacement for composition shingle, metal, tile, clay, concrete and flat or hot mop roofing systems. We inspect the existing structure, walk you through material options suited to your home and the coastal climate, and take our time installing it — we don't rush the details that determine whether a roof lasts 15 years or 40.

Signs It's Time

When to replace.

Roof Is Near or Past Its Lifespan
Composition shingle roofs in coastal fog and salt air often show wear sooner than the same material inland. If your roof is old and worn broadly, replacement is usually the smarter spend.
Repeated Repairs
If you've called a roofer more than once or twice in the last couple years for the same section, the material is likely failing across the board, not just at one spot.
Widespread Curling or Cracking
Shingles curling at the edges, granule loss, or cracked tile across large sections of the roof point to a material that's simply worn out.
Selling or Refinancing
A tired roof shows up in home inspections and can affect financing or sale price. A replacement resets the clock and gives buyers confidence.

Common Questions

Got questions?

How do I know it's time to replace my roof instead of repairing it?+
If the roofing material is near or past its expected lifespan, if damage is spread across multiple areas, or if you've needed several repairs in the past couple years, replacement is usually the better long-term value. We inspect the full roof and give you an honest comparison of repair versus replacement cost before you decide.
How long does a roof replacement take?+
Timelines vary by roof size, material and complexity. We'll give you a specific estimate for your project during the free estimate visit. We take the time needed to install it correctly rather than rushing to hit an arbitrary one-day timeline.
What roofing materials do you install?+
We install composition shingle, metal, tile, clay, concrete and flat or hot mop roofing systems. We'll help you weigh cost, lifespan and performance in coastal conditions for your specific home.
Do you handle permits for a full roof replacement?+
Roof replacements in Watsonville and surrounding jurisdictions typically require a permit and inspection. Ask us about the permitting process for your project when you request your estimate.
